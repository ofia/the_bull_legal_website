3D Digital Twins
Every property is a live 3D model. Switch between ARCHI (blueprint) and TRON (wireframe) views. Navigate by floor or unit.
Tenant Management
Full tenant profiles, lease tracking, payment history, and automated rent reminders. Every relationship in one place.
Financial Dashboard
Revenue, expenses, and cash flow per property and across the full portfolio. Live figures, not month-end spreadsheets.
