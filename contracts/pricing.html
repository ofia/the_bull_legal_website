Common questions
Can I switch plans later?
Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.
What counts as a contract?
Any document you upload and analyze through the platform. Draft versions and archived contracts count toward your limit.
Is my data secure?
Yes. We use Anthropic's Zero Data Retention API tier — your contract content is never logged by any AI provider. All data is scoped to your firm and encrypted at rest. Learn more →
Do you offer a DPA for compliance?
Data Processing Agreements are available on Enterprise plans. Contact us to request one.
How do I cancel?
You can cancel anytime from your account settings. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period, with no further charges.
