Process

Contract in.
Clarity out.

Upload any agreement. The AI reads it, maps every obligation, and hands the legal team a live dashboard — not a PDF.

01 — Ingest
Upload the contract
PDF, DOCX, or plain text. The system ingests it and begins extraction immediately.
02 — Extract
AI maps every node
Claude identifies all parties, payments, deadlines, terms, and documents — then turns them into a live process graph.
03 — Track
Status updates automatically
Incoming emails, uploads, and confirmations are scanned. Nodes flip from pending → confirmed without anyone touching a keyboard.
04 — Act
Reminders fire on their own
Deadline approaching? The platform emails the right party. Payment overdue? The lawyer gets an alert. Zero manual follow-up.
